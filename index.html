Welcome to the USDA Foods Analysis Dashboard

Discover insights into ultra-processed foods, nutritional trends, and hidden patterns within our food data.

This project dives deep into USDA food data, exploring nutritional values, ingredient composition, and the impact of ultra-processed foods on health. Utilizing advanced data science techniques like Principal Component Analysis (PCA), clustering, and predictive modeling, our interactive dashboard offers a comprehensive view of food data insights. Whether you're a nutritionist, researcher, or simply curious about what you eat, you'll find valuable information here!

Click on the tabs above to explore each section of our project:

  • Data Preparation: Understand how we cleaned and transformed raw data.
  • Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA): Visualize food trends and patterns.
  • PCA & Feature Analysis: Discover key nutritional components and reduce data complexity.
  • Modeling & Predictions: See how machine learning models predict food categorization.
  • Conclusions: Key takeaways and actionable insights from our analysis.
